Projects

SuperSonic Drive-In

During the COVID 19 pandemic resturants are struggling to keep business a float and customers miss the dining experience. This program will solve that problem. Like the fast food chain Sonic Drive-In customers are allowed to eat in their car, however this program will allow customers to order and receive food and service without contact.

H.E.L.P Hiking Made Easy

Many of the gyms are closed, it is difficult to find a place to exercise and buying gym equipment is too expensive. This program allows users to look up hikes around Orange County and train there. It shows informaton on the hike and has a tracker to help with training.

Let's Play NBA Basketball

This program shows the importance of DFS, BFS, MST, and A* alorgithms. The application used these alorgithms to design a travel program that helps the user find a route from city to city for the NBA East and West Conferences.

European Vacation
